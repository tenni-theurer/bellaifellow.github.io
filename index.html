About the Bell AI Fellowship

Named after Alexander Graham Bell, inventor of the telephone, we are creating a new kind of digital experience—where voice is not just an interface, but a medium for connection, ritual, and transformation.

This is your invitation to join a highly selective, hands-on fellowship for early-career technologists, creators, and designers who want to shape how humans interact with AI through sound, story, and emotion.

This isn't a job. It's a call to invent a new medium—rooted in play, powered by empathy, and delivered through the most human interface we have: the voice.

Who We're Looking For

We're assembling a small, extraordinary cohort. What matters most is your creative instinct, technical curiosity, and emotional intelligence.

What You'll Work On

Fellows will build and contribute to live, growing systems used by real communities. Depending on your focus area, you might:

  • Design and prototype voice agents that lead gratitude rituals or creative collaborations
  • Connect automation flows (SMS → Voice → Web) using low/no-code tools and LLM APIs
  • Build voice agents for specific use cases: wellness coaches, creative collaborators, or community facilitators
  • Conduct user testing and iterate on emotionally intelligent interactions
  • Help shape the grammar of voice-first storytelling
