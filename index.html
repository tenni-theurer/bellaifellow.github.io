About the Bell AI Fellowship

Named after Alexander Graham Bell, inventor of the telephone, we are creating a new kind of digital experience, where voice is not just an interface, but a medium for connection, ritual, and transformation.

This is your invitation to join a highly selective, hands-on fellowship for technologists, creators, and designers at any stage of career who want to shape how humans interact with AI through sound, story, and emotion.

This isn't a job. It's a call to invent a new medium, rooted in play, powered by empathy, and delivered through the most human interface we have: the voice.

Fellowship Details

Our inaugural cohort brings together 4 to 6 exceptional individuals for an intensive 3-month program.

  • Duration: 3 months (September 3 to November 25, 2025)
  • Commitment: 20 hours per week, part-time
  • Stipend: $1,000 per month
  • Cohort Size: 4 to 6 carefully selected fellows
  • Location: Primarily remote, with the ability to attend 3 in-person sessions in the Bay Area during the program
  • Application Deadline: August 28, 2025
  • Investment: Gain experience, network, portfolio, and access to cutting-edge AI tools — no tuition required

Who We're Looking For

We're assembling a small, extraordinary cohort. What matters most is your creative instinct, technical curiosity, and emotional intelligence.

  • A technical builder or software engineer interested in AI voice applications and experience with relevant tools or languages
  • A content creator excited to tell stories or capture emotion through audio
  • An experience or game designer who wants to build interactive, conversational rituals
  • A UX thinker who believes voice is the most underexplored dimension of design
  • Or a curious, self-starting creative technologist who wants to push boundaries

What You'll Work On

Fellows will build and contribute to live, growing systems used by real communities. Depending on your focus area, you might:

  • Design and prototype voice agents that lead gratitude rituals or creative collaborations
  • Connect automation flows (SMS → Voice → Web) using low/no-code tools and LLM APIs
  • Build voice agents for specific use cases: wellness coaches, creative collaborators, or community facilitators
  • Conduct user testing and iterate on emotionally intelligent interactions
  • Help design voice-first AI where humans lead and AI assists
